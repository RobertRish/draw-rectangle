A Man in Love with NLP and Buddy's Pizza Coupons

There was once a man who loved two things more than anything else in the world: natural language processing and Buddy's Pizza coupons.

He spent his days studying NLP algorithms and his evenings poring over the latest deals on Buddy's Pizza. He had a shoebox full of coupons, each one a treasure he cherished and guarded fiercely.

Then one day he met a man named Hasan Piker. Hasan was nothing like him - he knew nothing about NLP and cared nothing for pizza coupons. But the two of them hit it off anyway, and before long they fell in love.

They eloped to a small town in the middle of nowhere, and there they lived happily ever after, NLP algorithms and pizza coupons forgotten.
